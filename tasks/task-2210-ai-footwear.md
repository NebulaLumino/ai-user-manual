# Task 2210: AI Footwear Design & Shoe Specification Generator
- **Status**: [✅] Not Started
- **Priority**: HIGH
- **Theme**: AI x Fashion, Apparel Design & Sustainable Style
- **App Dir**: ai-footwear-design
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-footwear-design
- **Description**: Generate footwear design concepts with construction details, material specs, and manufacturing guidance
- **Form Fields**: shoe type (sneaker/dress-boot/sandal/heels/sandals/loafer), target gender/age, end-use (athletic/dress/casual/work), material preference (leather/suede/synthetic/mesh), price tier, sustainability priority, construction method preference (cemented/Blake-stitched/Goodyear-welted)
- **Output**: Design concept description with silhouette, material specification sheet (upper/outsole/insole/lining), construction method detail, last shape recommendation, colorway options (3 variants), sizing/grading notes, cost estimate per pair at different volumes, manufacturing complexity assessment
