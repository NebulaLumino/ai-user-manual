# Task 2212: AI Jewelry Design & Gemstone Authentication Helper
- **Status**: [✅] Not Started
- **Priority**: MEDIUM
- **Theme**: AI x Fashion, Apparel Design & Sustainable Style
- **App Dir**: ai-jewelry-design
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-jewelry-design
- **Description**: Generate jewelry design concepts and assist with gemstone evaluation, metal authentication, and value estimation
- **Form Fields**: jewelry type (ring/pendant/earrings/bracelet), metal preference (gold/silver/platinum), gemstone interest (diamond/colored stone/pearl/none), budget tier, design aesthetic (minimalist/vintage/bohemian/bold), wear occasion (daily/special-occasion/bridal)
- **Output**: Design concept description with metal and stone specifications, gemstone quality assessment framework (4Cs for diamonds), metal hallmark identification guide, care and storage instructions, approximate retail value range, alternative design options within budget
