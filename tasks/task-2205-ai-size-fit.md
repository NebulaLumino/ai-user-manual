# Task 2205: AI Size & Fit Recommender & Body Measurement Analyzer
- **Status**: [✅] Not Started
- **Priority**: HIGH
- **Theme**: AI x Fashion, Apparel Design & Sustainable Style
- **App Dir**: ai-size-fit
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-size-fit
- **Description**: Analyze body measurements and recommend optimal sizes across brands, identify fit issues, and generate custom fit alteration suggestions
- **Form Fields**: height, weight, body type (hourglass/pear/apple/rectangle/inverted-triangle), measurement preference (cm or inches), target brands or garment types, common fit problems experienced, garment category (denim/dress-shirts/sportswear/formal-wear)
- **Output**: Size recommendation per brand or pattern, body proportion analysis, common fit problem solutions (e.g., "short waist adjustments"), alteration guide for purchased garments, size comparison chart across brands, personalized pattern grading notes
