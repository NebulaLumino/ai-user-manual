# Task 2277: AI Self-Compassion & Self-Esteem Building Program Generator
- **Status: [x] Complete
- **Priority**: MEDIUM
- **Theme**: AI x Mental Health, Wellness & Emotional Intelligence
- **App Dir**: ai-self-compassion
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-self-compassion
- **Description**: Generate self-compassion practices based on Kristin Neff's framework — common humanity, mindfulness, and self-kindness — for people with low self-worth
- **Form Fields**: current self-talk patterns (critical/perfectionist/people-pleasing/shame), situation causing distress, attachment style if known, experience with meditation (none/some/regular), willingness to try journaling vs. meditation vs. both, primary form self-criticism takes
- **Output**: Self-compassion letter to yourself based on your situation, self-compassion break exercise script (3-step: notice pain/talk to yourself like a friend/common humanity statement), reframing of the critical inner voice, compassionate acceptance statement, daily self-compassion practice guide (10 days), common humanity reminders, self-kindness vs. self-indulgence distinction, progress tracking
