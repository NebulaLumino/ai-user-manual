# Task 2304: AI Road Trip Route Planner & Campground Advisor
- **Status**: [✅] Complete
- **Priority**: MEDIUM
- **Theme**: AI x Travel, Tourism & Adventure Planning
- **App Dir**: ai-road-trip
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-road-trip
- **Description**: Plan scenic road trips with route optimization, campground recommendations, and stop planning for van life or camping trips
- **Form Fields**: start and end points (or loop route), time available, interests (scenic/cultural/food/wildlife/adventure), accommodation type (hotels/camping/both), vehicle type (car/RV/van/bike), season and weather considerations, must-see vs. flexible stops, fuel budget
- **Output**: Route map with daily driving distances, recommended stops per day with rationale, campground or accommodation recommendations per stop, scenic overlooks and photo stops, off-the-beaten-path alternatives, fuel cost estimate, road conditions and terrain notes, what to see if you have an extra day, packing list for the route type, driving tips and road rules for the region
