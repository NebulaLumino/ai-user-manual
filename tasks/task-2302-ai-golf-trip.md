# Task 2302: AI Golf Trip Planner & Course Rating Analyzer
- **Status**: [✅] Complete
- **Priority**: MEDIUM
- **Theme**: AI x Travel, Tourism & Adventure Planning
- **App Dir**: ai-golf-trip
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-golf-trip
- **Description**: Plan golf trips to bucket-list courses and destinations with tee time logistics and travel coordination
- **Form Fields**: target destinations or courses, handicap level, budget tier, travel dates flexibility, accommodation preference (resort/on-course/hotel), group size, must-play features (links/parkland/mountain/desert), club rental vs. bring-own-clubs
- **Output**: Course recommendations by destination with ranking rationale, tee time booking strategy and best times to play, travel logistics (flights + ground transport), accommodation recommendations near courses, estimated total trip cost, what to know before playing each course (strategy notes), club shipping vs. rental analysis, golf trip packing list, weather contingency planning
