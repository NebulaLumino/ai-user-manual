# Task 2204: AI Color Forecasting & Seasonal Palette Generator
- **Status**: [✅] Not Started
- **Priority**: HIGH
- **Theme**: AI x Fashion, Apparel Design & Sustainable Style
- **App Dir**: ai-color-forecasting
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-color-forecasting
- **Description**: Generate seasonal color palettes for fashion collections based on trend analysis, color theory, and market positioning
- **Form Fields**: season/year, product category (RTW/accessories/footwear/athleisure), target customer demographic, brand positioning (trendy/classic/sustainable/luxury), color story preference (monochromatic/complementary/analogous/earth/neon), existing brand color constraints
