# Task 2208: AI Clothing Carbon Footprint & Sustainability Score Calculator
- **Status**: [✅] Not Started
- **Priority**: HIGH
- **Theme**: AI x Fashion, Apparel Design & Sustainable Style
- **App Dir**: ai-fashion-carbon
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-fashion-carbon
- **Description**: Calculate the environmental footprint of garments — carbon emissions, water usage, waste, and ethical labor score
- **Form Fields**: garment type and fiber content, country of manufacture, brand sustainability certifications (GOTS/Oeko-Tex/FairTrade/B Corp), price tier, care method (hand-wash/cold-cycle/hot-cycle), expected garment lifespan (disposableInvestment), repairability interest
- **Output**: Carbon footprint estimate (kg CO2e), water usage estimate (liters), waste score, labor ethics rating, overall sustainability grade (A-F), care tips to reduce footprint, alternative more sustainable options, environmental impact comparison vs. similar items
