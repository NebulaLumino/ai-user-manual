# Task 2218: AI Bridal & Formal Wear Styling Advisor
- **Status**: [✅] Not Started
- **Priority**: MEDIUM
- **Theme**: AI x Fashion, Apparel Design & Sustainable Style
- **App Dir**: ai-bridal-styling
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-bridal-styling
- **Description**: Generate bridal styling plans, formal dress guides, and occasion-wear wardrobes for weddings, galas, and special events
- **Form Fields**: occasion type (wedding/bridesmaid/gala/black-tie/cocktail/graduation), dress code, body type and coverage preferences, cultural tradition requirements, budget tier, climate/season, reuse potential (multi-event wardrobe), accessories already owned
- **Output**: Dress style recommendations with silhouette, fabric and color guidance, accessories complete look, styling notes (what to wear under/how to move in it), alteration suggestions, outfit reuse strategy, rental vs. buy analysis, budget breakdown
