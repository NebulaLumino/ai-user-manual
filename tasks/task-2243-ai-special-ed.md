# Task 2243: AI Special Education IEP Generator & Accommodation Planner
- **Status**: ✅ Complete Not Started
- **Priority**: MEDIUM
- **Theme**: AI x Education, Learning & Academic Research
- **App Dir**: ai-special-ed
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-special-ed
- **Description**: Generate IEP (Individualized Education Program) goals, accommodation plans, and progress monitoring tools for students with learning differences
- **Form Fields**: student age/grade, disability category (ADHD/dyslexia/autism/ gifted+LD/other), grade-level performance gaps, school setting (mainstream/resource/specialized), available support staff, parent/teacher concerns, state standards alignment needed
- **Output**: Annual IEP goal statements (SMART format, 3-5 goals), quarterly benchmarks, classroom accommodations checklist (testing/behavioral/physical/seating), required assistive technology, progress monitoring data collection method, service minutes recommendation (OT/PT/speech/spec-ed), parent meeting agenda
