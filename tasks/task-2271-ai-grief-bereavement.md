# Task 2271: AI Grief & Bereavement Support Guide Generator
- **Status: [x] Complete
- **Priority**: MEDIUM
- **Theme**: AI x Mental Health, Wellness & Emotional Intelligence
- **App Dir**: ai-grief-support
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-grief-support
- **Description**: Generate grief support resources, bereavement timelines, and memorial planning guides for people processing loss
- **Form Fields**: type of loss (death of person/pet/relationship/fertility/dream), relationship to what was lost, time since loss, current stage (shock/acute grief/integration), cultural/religious tradition, available support (therapist/support-group/family/none), current primary grief symptoms
- **Output**: Grief timeline overview (what to expect in first weeks/months/years), personalized coping strategies by grief stage, memorial planning suggestions, what friends/family can do to help (shareable list), grief myths to reject, when to seek professional help guidance, continuing bonds activities, journaling prompts specific to your loss
