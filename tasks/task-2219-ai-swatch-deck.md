# Task 2219: AI Fashion Buyer Order Sheet & Line Planning Generator
- **Status**: [✅] Not Started
- **Priority**: MEDIUM
- **Theme**: AI x Fashion, Apparel Design & Sustainable Style
- **App Dir**: ai-line-planning
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-line-planning
- **Description**: Help fashion buyers build buy plans and line sheets — selecting styles, quantities, and price points for a retail buying season
- **Form Fields**: retail channel (boutique/department-store/multi-brand/online), target customer demographic, number of SKUs to buy, budget total, categories to buy (tops/bottoms/dresses/outerwear), brand preference (designer/brid//private-label), season/buying window
- **Output**: Line plan with category breakdown and budget allocation, key style recommendations by category, price-point architecture (entry/mid/premium ratio), quantity recommendations per SKU, open-to-buy calculator, markdown risk assessment, reorder vs. one-shot strategy
