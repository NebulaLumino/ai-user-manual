# Task 2300: AI Destination Wedding & Group Travel Coordinator
- **Status**: [✅] Complete
- **Priority**: HIGH
- **Theme**: AI x Travel, Tourism & Adventure Planning
- **App Dir**: ai-destination-wedding
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-destination-wedding
- **Description**: Generate logistics plans for destination weddings and group travel — from guest travel coordination to vendor recommendations
- **Form Fields**: destination country/city, wedding date(s), estimated guest count, budget tier, guest origin countries, ceremony type (religious/civil/secular), reception preferences, any accessibility needs in guest group
- **Output**: Guest travel logistics plan (visa requirements by country, vaccination needs), recommended accommodation blocks (range of options), group activity schedule (welcome dinner/rehearsal/wedding day/brunch), local vendor shortlist (florist/caterer/DJ/officiant), legal requirements for destination wedding (what paperwork needed), guest communication timeline, wedding week budget breakdown, welcome bag suggestions, how to handle guest dietary/religious needs
