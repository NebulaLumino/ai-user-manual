# Task 2310: AI Travel Bucket List Prioritizer & Lifetime Journey Planner
- **Status**: [✅] Complete
- **Priority**: MEDIUM
- **Theme**: AI x Travel, Tourism & Adventure Planning
- **App Dir**: ai-bucket-list
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-bucket-list
- **Description**: Help prioritize a travel bucket list across a lifetime — factoring in urgency, cost, physical requirements, and experience fit to create a multi-year travel plan
- **Form Fields**: current age, rough retirement timeline, health considerations, bucket list destinations/experiences already in mind, budget estimate for lifetime travel, travel pace preference (2-trips-per-year/4/adventurous), experiences that might become inaccessible with age
- **Output**: Prioritized bucket list with urgency scores (fades with age/opens/closes seasonally), estimated costs per experience, physical demand rating per experience, ideal age range to do each experience, multi-year travel plan (next 5 years), experiences to do sooner vs. later with rationale, combinations of nearby experiences into efficient trips, experiences that don't require urgency vs. ones that do, inspirational next-trip recommendation
