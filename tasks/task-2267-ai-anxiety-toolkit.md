# Task 2267: AI Anxiety Management Toolkit & Exposure Hierarchy Builder
- **Status: [x] Complete
- **Priority**: HIGH
- **Theme**: AI x Mental Health, Wellness & Emotional Intelligence
- **App Dir**: ai-anxiety-toolkit
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-anxiety-toolkit
- **Description**: Generate anxiety management plans, exposure hierarchies, and cognitive restructuring exercises for specific phobias or generalized anxiety
- **Form Fields**: anxiety type (GAD/social/phobia/PTSD/panic), main feared situations or thoughts, current avoidance behaviors, anxiety intensity (1-10 scale), previous coping attempts, preferred therapy modality (CBT/ERP/exposure/ACT), support available
- **Output**: Exposure hierarchy with 10 feared situations ranked by SUDS level, cognitive restructuring worksheet (identifying distorted thoughts and generating alternatives), behavioral experiment plan, anxiety tolerance tips for each hierarchy level, progress tracking chart, when to seek higher-level care guidance
