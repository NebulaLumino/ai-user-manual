# Task 2307: AI Ski Resort & Winter Sports Trip Planner
- **Status**: [✅] Complete
- **Priority**: MEDIUM
- **Theme**: AI x Travel, Tourism & Adventure Planning
- **App Dir**: ai-ski-resort
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-ski-resort
- **Description**: Plan ski trips, snowboard holidays, and winter adventure travel with resort comparisons and logistics
- **Form Fields**: skiing ability (beginner/intermediate/advanced/expert), preferred resort region (Alps/Colorado/Japan/Canada/Andes/etc.), trip duration, budget tier, group ability range (mixed abilities), equipment (rent/bring), preferred accommodation (ski-in-out/hotel/airbnb/shuttle)
- **Output**: Resort recommendations based on ability and budget, terrain breakdown and off-piste availability, ski pass comparison (lift tickets vs. multi-resort passes), equipment rental vs. bring strategy, accommodation recommendations per resort, après-ski options, off-slope activities for non-skiers, packing list for ski trip, shuttle/transport logistics from nearest airport, best time to book for optimal pricing
