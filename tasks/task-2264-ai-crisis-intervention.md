# Task 2264: AI Crisis Intervention Safety Plan & Support Network Builder
- **Status: [x] Complete
- **Priority**: HIGH
- **Theme**: AI x Mental Health, Wellness & Emotional Intelligence
- **App Dir**: ai-crisis-plan
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-crisis-plan
- **Description**: Generate a personalized crisis safety plan for people with suicidal ideation, self-harm urges, or severe emotional crises
- **Form Fields**: current crisis level (active ideation/passing thoughts/heightened emotion/prevention planning), warning signs they notice, coping strategies that usually help, reasons for living/personal values, trusted support people and their contact info, professional resources available, environment safety items to secure
- **Output**: Complete safety plan document with warning signs, coping strategies ranked by effectiveness, reasons for living prominently displayed, support network contact list, professional resource list (988 Lifeline, Crisis Text Line, local ER), environmental safety steps, reassessment schedule, what to tell trusted people to say/do, My Reasons for Living statement
