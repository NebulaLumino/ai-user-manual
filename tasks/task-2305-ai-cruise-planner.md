# Task 2305: AI Cruise Ship & Multi-Port Destination Planner
- **Status**: [✅] Complete
- **Priority**: MEDIUM
- **Theme**: AI x Travel, Tourism & Adventure Planning
- **App Dir**: ai-cruise-planner
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-cruise-planner
- **Description**: Plan cruise itineraries and multi-port travel with shore excursion recommendations and port logistics
- **Form Fields**: cruise line preference, itinerary (Mediterranean/Caribbean/Alaska/Asia/Northern Europe/etc.), number of days, ship class preference, budget tier, travel style (relaxation/active/adventure/mixed), ports visited, dietary requirements, mobility considerations
- **Output**: Port-by-port activity recommendations (guided tours/self-guided/discovery), best-value shore excursions per port, dining strategy (ship vs. ashore), optimal time in each port, packing strategy for multi-climate itineraries, pre-cruise and post-cruise city guide for embarkation/disembarkation cities, ship activity schedule optimization (avoiding FOMO), travel insurance considerations for cruises, gratuity and hidden cost planning
