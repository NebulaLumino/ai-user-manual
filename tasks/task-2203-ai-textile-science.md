# Task 2203: AI Textile Selection Advisor & Fabric Performance Analyzer
- **Status**: [✅] Not Started
- **Priority**: HIGH
- **Theme**: AI x Fashion, Apparel Design & Sustainable Style
- **App Dir**: ai-textile-science
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-textile-science
- **Description**: Match fabrics to garment designs and end-uses based on fiber properties, weave structure, and performance characteristics
- **Form Fields**: garment end-use, required properties (stretch/water-resistance/breathability/durability), care requirements, sustainability priority, budget tier, fiber preferences (natural/synthetic/blend), country of manufacture constraints
- **Output**: Fabric recommendation with fiber content and weave structure, performance attribute analysis, care instructions, sustainability assessment (scorecard), cost estimate per yard/meter, supplier type recommendations, alternative options ranked
