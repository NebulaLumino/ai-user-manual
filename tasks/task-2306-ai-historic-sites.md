# Task 2306: AI Historic Site Guide & Archaeological Tourism Planner
- **Status**: [✅] Complete
- **Priority**: MEDIUM
- **Theme**: AI x Travel, Tourism & Adventure Planning
- **App Dir**: ai-historic-sites
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-historic-sites
- **Description**: Plan trips centered on historical and archaeological sites with context, timing, and expert guide recommendations
- **Form Fields**: historical period of interest (ancient-Greek/ancient-Roman/medieval/Renaissance/World-War/etc.), regions/countries, physical fitness level for climbing/trekking, time available, depth preference (overview/tourist-attention/deep-scholar), accessibility needs
- **Output**: Site recommendations ranked by historical significance and experience level, best time to visit each site to avoid crowds, recommended guide/archaeologist tour services, historical context briefing per site (what happened there, why it matters), physical requirements and accessibility notes, suggested combining sites into logical routes, reading/watching recommendations before visiting, photography tips for each site, nearby lesser-known sites to pair with major attractions
