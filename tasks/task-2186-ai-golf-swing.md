- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-golf-strategy
- **Description**: Generate personalized golf improvement plans and course-specific strategy guides based on handicap, club speed, and course characteristics
- **Form Fields**: handicap index, current average score, driving distance, weakest part of game, course being played (or typical course characteristics), weather conditions, tournament/casual round, mental game priority
