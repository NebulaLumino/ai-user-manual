# Task 2209: AI Fashion Trend Research & Competitor Collection Analyzer
- **Status**: [✅] Not Started
- **Priority**: HIGH
- **Theme**: AI x Fashion, Apparel Design & Sustainable Style
- **App Dir**: ai-fashion-research
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-fashion-research
- **Description**: Analyze competitor collections, fashion week shows, and trend reports to generate competitive intelligence for brand buyers and designers
- **Form Fields**: target brand or designer, competitor set to analyze, season/collection to investigate, analysis goal (mood/silhouette/color/trend/demand), your brand positioning, retail channel (luxury/mid-mass/fast-fashion/direct-to-consumer)
- **Output**: Key trend themes identified, silhouette and shape analysis, color story extraction, fabric/material innovation notes, price architecture analysis, demand signals from runway to retail, white space opportunities for buyer's own collection, trend confidence rating
