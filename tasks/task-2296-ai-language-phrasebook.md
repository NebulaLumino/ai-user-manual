# Task 2296: AI Language Phrasebook & Travel Conversation Helper
- **Status**: [✅] Complete
- **Priority**: HIGH
- **Theme**: AI x Travel, Tourism & Adventure Planning
- **App Dir**: ai-language-phrasebook
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-language-phrasebook
- **Description**: Generate a travel phrasebook with essential phrases, pronunciation guides, and cultural context for any language
- **Form Fields**: target language, travel scenario (arrival/hotel/restaurant/shopping/transport/emergency/medical), proficiency level (complete beginner/know-some/basic), dialect preference if applicable, special needs (traveling with kids/elderly/disabled)
- **Output**: 50 essential phrases organized by scenario with phonetic pronunciation, numbers and counting guide, yes/no and basic response phrases, food-specific restaurant phrases, emergency phrases with local emergency numbers, transportation must-know phrases, body language and gesture notes (some are offensive in this culture), polite vs. casual forms, how to ask for help
