# Task 2236: AI Exam Preparation Planner & Quiz Generator
- **Status**: ✅ Complete Not Started
- **Priority**: HIGH
- **Theme**: AI x Education, Learning & Academic Research
- **App Dir**: ai-exam-prep
- **GitHub**: https://github.com/NebulaLumino/ai-exam-prep
- **Description**: Generate personalized exam study plans, practice quizzes, and test-taking strategies based on subject, exam date, and available study time
- **Form Fields**: exam name/type, subject area, exam date, hours available to study total, current knowledge level (confident/struggling/incomplete), topic areas to focus on or skip, exam format (multiple-choice/essay/mixed/practical), number of practice questions needed
- **Output**: Study schedule (daily breakdown from now to exam), priority ranking of topics to study, 20+ practice questions with answer key, test-taking strategy tips, what to do the night before and morning of, estimated score range if practice goes well/poorly, red-flag topics requiring extra attention
